DEVELOPMENT COVENANTS

c

Construction Requirements:

Buildings to the value of not less than ₦100,000.00 (One Hundred Thousand Naira) must be erected to the satisfaction of and in accordance with plans approved by Enugu North Authority or the Governor's appointed officer.

a

Construction Restrictions:

No building shall be erected other than those covenanted in this Certificate. No additions or alterations permitted without approved plans and specifications from the Enugu North Authority.

i

Sanitation Infrastructure:

Installation and operation of a water-borne sewage system required within six months of connection to piped water supply.

USAGE & CONDUCT COVENANTS

c

Usage Restrictions:

Buildings shall not be used for any purpose other than specified in this Certificate.

e

Nuisance Prohibition:

No noxious, noisy, or offensive activities that may inconvenience adjoining occupants.

f

Living Standards:

Must maintain accommodation and sanitary conditions conforming to neighborhood standards.

b

Maintenance Obligation:

All buildings and appurtenances must be kept in good and tenantable repair.

TRANSFER & LEGAL COVENANTS

d

Alienation Restrictions:

The right of occupancy shall not be alienated by sale, assignment, mortgage, transfer of possession, sublease or bequest without prior Governor's consent.

j

All rent payments and covenants remain binding on successors, assigns, and transferees.
